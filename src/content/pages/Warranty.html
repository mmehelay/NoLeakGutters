5-year workmanship warranty, 10 year corner warranty, 20 year material warranty

At No Leak Gutters Inc, we are dedicated to providing you with top-quality gutter solutions that stand the test of time. With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, we are proud to offer comprehensive warranties to ensure your peace of mind.

5-Year Workmanship Warranty

  • Covers defects directly caused by our installation process, such as scratches, tears, and dents or improper installation.
  • Excludes damages from external factors, neglect, and modifications by third parties.

10-Year Leaking Corners Warranty

  • Ensures quality installations with #900 SOLAR SEAL adhesive with 20+ year expectancy.
  • Excludes changes to home impacting gutters such as roof replacement, and failure to maintain gutter cleanliness.

20-Year Material Warranty

  • Offered by and backed by our material suppliers
  • Guarantees longevity and performance of high-grade materials.
  • Covers resistance to rust, splitting, or cracking.

Claims Process

  • Submit written notice of the claim, copy of original contract, picture of alleged defect, and statement within 30 days.
  • Our team will assess eligibility and arrange repairs/replacements at no extra cost if covered.
  • Terms and conditions apply. Refer to full warranty document for details.
